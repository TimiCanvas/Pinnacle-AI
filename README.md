# Pinnacle-AI  
### AI-Powered Personalization for Modern Banking  

Pinnacle-AI is an intelligent personalization engine that makes Nigerian banking *deeply human again*.  
Built for the Zenith Bank hackathon, it helps banks like **Zenith Bank** understand each customer’s unique financial journey — delivering the **right product, at the right time, in the right language**, across mobile, web, USSD, and WhatsApp.

---

## Key Features  

- **Smart Customer Intelligence**  
  Detects life events such as graduations, relocations, or business launches from transaction and behavioral data.  

- **Predictive Recommendations**  
  A “Next-Best-Action” engine that predicts customer needs and recommends relevant financial products.  

- **Omnichannel Delivery**  
  Personalized experiences across mobile apps, websites, email, SMS, WhatsApp, and in-branch interactions.  

- **Localized for Nigeria**  
  Supports English, Pidgin, Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa — with integrations for BVN and NIBSS instant payment tracking.  

- **Privacy-First AI**  
  Designed with responsible data governance, customer consent, and security at its core.
